FAQ

Common questions

What exactly is EpicSoft?
EpicSoft is a platform where traffic teams can purchase fully managed instances of proven tools — starting with EpicPWA, with more products launching soon. Think of it as a marketplace for managed traffic infrastructure.
Do I need technical knowledge?
No. We handle all server management, updates, and infrastructure. You use the product through a clean admin panel — same as any SaaS tool.
What's included in the price?
Everything. Dedicated Hetzner server, SSL certificates, automated backups, monitoring, updates, and support. No hidden fees, no extra hosting costs.
Can I run multiple products?
Yes. Each product is a separate instance. You can run EpicPWA and EpicAds (when available) simultaneously, each on their own dedicated server.
How fast is setup?
Under 5 minutes. After payment, your instance is provisioned automatically. Connect your domain and you're live.
Is there a free trial?
No free trial since each instance requires dedicated server provisioning. Monthly plans have no lock-in — cancel anytime.
Will more products be added?
Yes. We're building a full suite of managed tools for traffic teams. Subscribe to get notified when new products launch.
